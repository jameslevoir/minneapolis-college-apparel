Ava Huge

Daisies

Designer Bio

Ava Huge has always had an interest in the fashion industry. She began modeling in 2021 and plans to continue modeling after graduation. She would like to further her career in the fashion industry as a fashion stylist and creative director. The Apparel Technologies program at Minneapolis College has helped her to understand how garments are made and has taught her many valuable industry skills. Some of her favorite skills learned include draping and fashion illustration.

Designer Statement

My collection, "Daisies," is a versatile collection of elegant streetwear, suitable for everyday wear or formal events. This collection showcases my minimalist aesthetic, through the use of clean lines and simple silhouettes. The looks are elevated with a curated selection of embellishments and accessories. I used a range of fabrics with unique textures and subtle patterns to further enhance the garments. The color story is made up of cool blues and purples with silver accents.
