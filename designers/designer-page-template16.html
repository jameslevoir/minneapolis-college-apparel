Melanie Lee

Zoo Nkauj Design

Designer Bio

Melanie was born in north Minneapolis. Melanie loves to sketch wedding gowns and hopes to become a designer for bridal and special occasion wear. Growing up, her mother collected traditional Hmong clothing to give to her daughters as a wedding gift, which inspired her to design her own garments.

Designer Statement

My collection “Zoo Nkauj Design,” which means beautiful designs in Hmong, is inspired by traditional Hmong clothing. The garments are paired with traditional Hmong accessories to complete the looks. With this collection, I hope to create garments that blend modern and traditional styles.
